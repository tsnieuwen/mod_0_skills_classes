Class: Pie

Attributes:
temperature (string)
calories (integer)
ingredients (array)
is_vegan (boolean)

Methods:
add_flavor (adds butter to ingredients if not already included. also increase calories by 300)
order_vegan (removes "egg", "milk" or "butter" from ingredients. changes is_vegan to true it not already true)
over_bake (will increase temperature)
a_la_mode: (served with ice cream, decreases temperature)
