Class: House

Attributes:
type_of_home (string)
number_of_rooms (integer)
home_value (integer)
has_fireplace (boolean)

Methods

construction (increases number of rooms by 1. increases home_value 15000)
remodel (changes has_fireplace to true, if false. increases home_value by 5000)
natural_disaster (reduces home_value by 50%)
paint_house (increase home_value by 500)
